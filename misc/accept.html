Technology Choices

Acceptance

Future developments for robotic companions for the elderly would most likely include the implementation of social and psychological aspects for the robot itself, as there is some evidence of elderly people unwilling to accept help electronically. The more human-like the robot is (in terms of conversation) the more enjoyable it will be for the user. However, this would require a lot of testing and design, as the robot would need to be socially intelligent, possibly recognize social cues and respond appropriately.
