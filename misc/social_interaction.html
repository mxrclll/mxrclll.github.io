Risks

Social Interaction

First of all, when we make robots to complete people's work at a cheaper cost, the contact between people has been greatly reduced. Robots take on more and more workloads and caregiving responsibilities, which may greatly reduce the elderly Level of interpersonal communication. Robots can work 24 hours a day to take care of the elderly, and it may be convenient to feed the vulnerable elderly with an automatic spoon, but this will eliminate the opportunity for meticulous and careful interpersonal communication between people. When we let robots take care of parents and grandparents, we may feel that we do not need to visit them so many times because they have found the company they need. In fact, this would be a mistake. For many elderly people, old age Human-robot interaction is less emotionally satisfying than human interaction, because it feels that what the robot expresses or does is not "real", nor is it based on realistic thoughts and feelings.
